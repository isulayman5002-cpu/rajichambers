01 — The Firm

A chambers built on the rigour
of the brief.

Raji Chambers is the law practice of Musibau Adetunbi, SAN — a Senior Advocate of Nigeria and Fellow of the Chartered Institute of Arbitrators (UK).

From offices in Ibadan and Abuja, the firm advises individuals, businesses, and institutions across four disciplines: high-stakes litigation, corporate and commercial transactions, election petition advocacy, and real estate counsel.

Our work is shaped by a single conviction — that the brief is sacred. Every matter, from a commercial dispute to a constitutional challenge, is met with the same standard of preparation, judgement, and resolve. We do not crowd our docket. We work the briefs we accept.

Led by a founding partner with rights of audience before every court in Nigeria and arbitral experience reaching the United Kingdom, the chambers brings together five partners with complementary expertise across litigation, transactions, and election jurisprudence.

“A practice is measured not by the cases it takes, but by the cases it wins on the strength of its preparation.” — House Standard
02 — Practice Areas

Four disciplines.
One standard.

I.

Litigation & Dispute Resolution

We represent clients in civil and commercial disputes before every level of the Nigerian court system, from the Magistracy to the Supreme Court, and in arbitral proceedings under domestic and international rules. Our litigators are trained for the long arc of a matter — from first instance through appeal — and bring the discipline of preparation that contested briefs demand.

II.

Corporate & Commercial Law

Advisory and transactional counsel for businesses at every stage — incorporation, governance, commercial contracts, mergers, joint ventures, regulatory compliance, and dispute prevention. We act as outside general counsel to companies that need the discipline of a chambers without the cost of in-house legal infrastructure.

III.

Pre-Election Suits & Election Petitions

A dedicated election jurisprudence practice covering pre-election suits, qualification challenges, party primaries litigation, and post-election petitions before the Election Petition Tribunals and Courts of Appeal. We act for candidates, political parties, and stakeholders in matters where constitutional time-bars and evidential rigour are decisive.

IV.

Real Estate & Property Management

Comprehensive property counsel covering title investigation, conveyancing, lease and tenancy structuring, land use and zoning, and the management of estates and investment property. We advise developers, landlords, families, and institutional holders on transactions and on the continuing administration of real assets.
